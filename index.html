    Strong password rules

    A safe password is long, unique, unpredictable, and not reused across accounts. This checker runs completely in your browser and does not store or upload passwords.

    Use length Prefer at least 12 to 16 characters.
    Mix character types Combine uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.
    Avoid patterns Skip names, dates, keyboard sequences, and common words.
    Use a manager Generate a unique password for every important account.
